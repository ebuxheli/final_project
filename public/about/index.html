About

Hello! My name is Enxhi Buxheli and I am currently a junior at Harvard College concentrating in Physics. I am fascinated by data and all the stories that you can tell using with it. The purpose of this specific website is to tell the story of the Russian Trolls in the 2016 US Presidential Election. The data used to form these stories came from a join FiveThirtyEight and Clemson University dataset. FiveThirtyEight also inspired me to create an article in similar spirit to their article on this same topic.

I hope that you enjoy this website and please reach out to me at ebuxheli at college dot harvard dot edu if you have any questions about the data or articles!
